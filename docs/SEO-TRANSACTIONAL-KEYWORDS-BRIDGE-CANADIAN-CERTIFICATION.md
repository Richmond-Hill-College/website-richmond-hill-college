## Content and page strategy

1. **Hub page: Bridge Canadian Certification** (`/bridge-canadian-certification`)  
   - Targets: “bridge canadian certification”, “bridge to canadian certification”, “canadian certification bridging program”.  
   - Content: What it means, why it matters, how RHC programs bridge to Canadian credentials, clear CTA to programs and contact/enroll.

2. **IEP-focused page: Canadian Certification for Internationally Educated** (`/canadian-certification-internationally-educated`)  
   - Targets: “canadian certification for internationally educated”, “internationally educated professionals canada certification”, “get canadian certification internationally trained”.  
   - Content: Who it’s for, benefits, program categories, pathways (PSW, pharmacy, pet grooming, etc.), CTA to courses and contact.

3. **Existing pages to cross-link:**  
   - `/bridging-programs` – link to both new pages in intro or “Learn more” section.  
   - `/programs`, `/course-offerings`, `/courses` – add contextual links to “Bridge to Canadian certification” and “Canadian certification for internationally educated professionals” where relevant.

4. **CTAs on every SEO page:**  
   - “View programs” → `/bridging-programs` or `/courses`.  
   - “Enroll / Register” → RHC Global Bridge or `/contact`.  
   - “Contact us” → `/contact`.

- Organic traffic to `/bridge-canadian-certification` and `/canadian-certification-internationally-educated`.

- Rankings for target phrases above (track in GSC/SEMrush/Ahrefs).  
- Conversions: form submissions and course registrations attributed to these URLs.
